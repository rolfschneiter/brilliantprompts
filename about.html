About BrilliantPrompts

We believe the biggest gap in AI isn't the models — it's the prompts.

The Problem

AI models like ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini are incredibly powerful. But most people use them with vague, unstructured prompts and get mediocre results. The difference between a good and great output often comes down to how you ask.

Our Solution

BrilliantPrompts is a curated library of 200+ expert-crafted prompts, each designed using proven prompt engineering techniques: role assignment, chain-of-thought reasoning, structured outputs, and few-shot examples.

Every prompt is tested across multiple AI models, includes practical usage guides, example outputs, and pro tips — so you get the best results, every time.

What Sets Us Apart

  • Tested & Verified — Every prompt is tested on GPT-4, Claude, and Gemini
  • Structured Format — Usage guide, example output, and pro tips with every prompt
  • Updated Weekly — 10-15 new prompts every week across all categories
  • 8 Categories — From coding to creative writing, business to image generation
  • One-Click Copy — Copy any prompt instantly to use in your favorite AI tool

Built for Professionals

Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2025

Overview

BrilliantPrompts ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website at rsx.ch.

Information We Collect

Payment Information: Payments are processed by Lemon Squeezy, our Merchant of Record. We do not store credit card numbers or bank details. Lemon Squeezy handles all payment data in accordance with PCI DSS standards.

License Keys: When you subscribe, a license key is generated and stored in your browser's local storage for authentication purposes. This key is validated against Lemon Squeezy's API.

Analytics: We may use privacy-friendly analytics to understand site usage (page views, popular categories). We do not track individual users or use cookies for advertising.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide access to our prompt library
  • To validate your subscription status
  • To improve our content and user experience
  • To send transactional emails (receipts, license keys)

Data Storage

Our website is a static site hosted on Netlify. We do not operate a database or store personal data on our servers. Your license key is stored only in your browser's local storage.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Lemon Squeezy — Payment processing and subscription management
  • Netlify — Website hosting
  • Google Fonts — Typography (Inter, JetBrains Mono)

Cookies

We do not use tracking cookies. The only data stored in your browser is your license key in local storage, which is essential for the service to function.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data held by Lemon Squeezy
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Cancel your subscription at any time
  • Clear your local storage to remove the license key from your browser

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

For privacy-related questions, please reach out through our customer portal or the contact details on our About page.
